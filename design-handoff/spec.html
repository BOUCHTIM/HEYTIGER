HEY TIGER00 · How to read this

HOW TO READ THIS

The site is built fluid & responsive — it reshapes itself between phone and desktop. Sizes below are written as min → max. Read this first; it makes every later page make sense.

Fluid sizes — clamp(min, ideal, max)

Almost nothing on this site is a single fixed number. A value like clamp(24px, 5vw, 72px) means: never smaller than 24px (phone), scales with screen width, never larger than 72px (large desktop).

  • Design the desktop artboard at the MAX value.
  • Design the mobile artboard at the MIN value.
  • vw = 1% of screen width · dvh = 1% of screen height.

How the wireframes work

  • red pill = a hard measurement (px / mm).
  • RED ZONE = an editable area — where you can place / swap artwork.
  • LOGO = fixed logo position.
  • Numbered 1 dots map to the notes beside each diagram.

HEY TIGER01 · Canvas & breakpoints

CANVAS & BREAKPOINTS

Design at these four artboard widths. Page background & photography bleed edge-to-edge; text content stays inside a 1360px max-width and is centred on big screens.

Recommended artboards

Desktop

primary
1440 × 900 px

The main design target. Content column maxes at 1360px; hero text column maxes at 1100px. Background photos fill the full width.

Large desktop

1920 × 1080 px

Only backgrounds / panorama stretch wider. Text + UI stay centred at ≤1360px, so margins grow on the sides.

Tablet

834 × 1112 px

Most 2-column splits have collapsed or are about to. Good checkpoint for the 900px & 768px reflows.

Mobile

primary
390 × 844 px

iPhone 14/15 class. Everything is single-column; the nav becomes a hamburger; a floating Book a table pill sits bottom-centre.

Letter-spacing (tracking)

Headlines are tight −0.01em. Labels & eyebrows are wide: 0.20em → 0.52em. The widest "ultra" tracking 0.44em is the signature look on small uppercase labels. Headlines, CTAs & section titles are UPPERCASE.

HEY TIGER04 · Spacing, grid & motion

SPACING & GRID

Consistent padding tokens keep the rhythm. These are the same values reused on every page edge and between blocks.

Spacing tokens
Section — X
clamp(24px → 72px)   Left/right padding on every full section.
Section — Y
clamp(48px → 88px)   Top/bottom padding on sections.
Section Y — LG
clamp(64px → 112px)   Larger top/bottom (e.g. Menu header).
Content — Y
clamp(32px → 52px)   Gaps between content blocks.
Content max-width
1360px centred (Form, Spaces). Hero text column 1100px.
Copy measure
Paragraphs capped at 40–56 characters per line for readability.
Corners & borders
  • Everything is square — 0 radius (cards, photos, inputs, tags).
  • Exception: pill buttons are fully round (999px).
  • Hairlines / dividers: 1px in Dark Border #2A1F2A.
  • Menu page header underline: 4px Tiger Red.
  • Spaces private-events block: 8px cream left bar.
Motion (for reference)
  • Fast 160ms · Base 280ms · Slow 600ms.
  • Standard ease cubic-bezier(.4,0,.2,1).
  • Cinematic ease cubic-bezier(.22,1,.36,1) for reveals.
  • Headlines have a subtle neon flicker; honour "reduce motion".
  • Hero background = slow 90s panorama pan across 5 photos.
Photo treatment (so new photos match)

Photos are darkened & slightly desaturated so cream/red text sits on top. Typical recipe: contrast 1.05–1.1 · saturate 0.7–0.9 · brightness 0.55–0.82, plus a dark gradient scrim at the bottom and (in some sections) an Amber Wood #C17B3F multiply tint at ~22–28%. Supply photos bright & punchy — the layout dims them.

Export specs
  • Logos & stickers: PNG, transparent, @2–3× the max render size. Also supply SVG if vector exists.
  • Photos: JPG, sRGB, quality ~80. Width ≥ 2400px for hero/full-bleed; ≥ 1200px for cards/strip.

  • Favicon / app icon: dedicated square 512×512 master (currently missing).

  • Keep square corners; only pill buttons are round.
Naming & handoff
  • Photos already live in /public/images/ → header · spaces · story · brand.
  • Logos & stickers live at /public/ root.
  • Match existing names when swapping (e.g. spaces/rooftop.jpg), or hand me a clearly-labelled folder and I'll wire it up.
  • Flag any colour that must print exactly — give me Pantone/CMYK too.
