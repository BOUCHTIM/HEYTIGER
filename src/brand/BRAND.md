```css
--font-extended:  'Jandus RD Extended', 'Jandus RD', ...
--font-condensed: 'Jandus RD Condensed', 'Jandus RD', ...
--font-jp:        'Dela Gothic One', 'Noto Sans JP', ...
--font-jp-rough:  'TA Kakugo', 'Dela Gothic One', 'Noto Sans JP', ...
```

### Japanese Text Styles
- **ヘイ、タイガー** (Hei, Taigaa) — Katakana Cool, pop-culture, brand-like → `font-jp`
- **ヘイタイガー** (direct transliteration) — modern, stylish → `font-jp`  
- **おいトラ** (Oi Tora) — rough/street attitude, cinematic → `font-jp-rough`
